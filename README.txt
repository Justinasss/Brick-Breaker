# Brick-Breaker
Brick Breaker is a classic arcade-style game where players control a paddle to bounce a ball and break a wall of bricks. The goal is to destroy all the bricks without letting the ball fall past the paddle. As the game progresses, it often gets faster and includes power-ups or special bricks, adding to the challenge and excitement.

Controls: a to move the platform leaft and d move the platform right
